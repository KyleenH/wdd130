Overview

Purpose

Hello, my name is Kyleen Hunter, I am the proud owner of Rafting Momentum located in The Dalles, Oregon. At Rafting Momentum we strive to provide you once in a lifetime white water rafting experiences. We have paths that vary anywhere from 1-4 hours to complete.

Audience

Do you love adventure? Do you like having experiences that turn into fond memories? At Rafting Momentum our goal is to provide safe and exciting rafting paths for all ages. We have slower paths for families with children as young as 4, we also have paths for people 18 and older with more aggresive waves for thrill seekers. Water rafting is a great form of exercise and bonding experience. Book an event today!
