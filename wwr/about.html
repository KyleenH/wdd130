History

I grew up in Vancouver, Washington which is about a 45 minute drive away from The Dalles, Oregon. I grew up going on hikes all over the gorge, hikes like Multnomah Falls, Angels' Rest and Punchbowl Falls. I first came up with the idea of starting a rafting trip company when I was 20, with the help and support of several friends and family members my dream became a reality. I hope that you fall in love with the wildlife of Oregon like I did when I was little. -Kyleen Hunter, Founder and Owner of Rafting Momentum
